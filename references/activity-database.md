# Activity Database

_Load during Phase 5 — Timeline Generation. Filter by trip type, group profile, and budget tier._

---

## How to Use This File

1. Find the primary trip type section
2. Select activities matching the group profile and budget tier
3. For each day, aim for: 1 high-energy activity, 1 cultural/relaxing activity, 1 food experience
4. Always include 1 rainy-day alternative per day
5. Adapt timing: active activities in morning, relaxed in afternoon, entertainment in evening

---

## ACTIVITY STRUCTURE FORMAT

When building each day, use this format for each activity:

```
[Activity Name]
  Type: Physical / Cultural / Food / Entertainment / Nature / Shopping
  Duration: X hours
  Best Time: Morning / Afternoon / Evening / Any
  Cost Range: Budget ($1–5) / Mid ($10–30) / Premium ($50–100) / Luxury ($100+)
  Group Fit: Solo / Couple / Friends / Family-Kids / Large Group / Any
  Book Ahead: Yes / No / Recommended
  Tip: [1 specific insider tip]
  Rainy Alternative: [What to do instead]
```

## BEACH / RESORT ACTIVITIES

### Morning (High Energy)
- **Sunrise beach walk** | Nature | 1h | Free | Any | No | Best light for photos; almost no crowds
- **Snorkeling at reef** | Physical | 2–3h | Mid ($10–20 gear rental) | Any except very young kids | No | Go before 10am, visibility best
- **Surfing lesson** | Physical/Sport | 2h | Mid ($25–40 incl. board) | Friends, Teens, Adults | Yes (peak season) | Book certified school, not beach touts
- **Stand-up paddleboard** | Physical | 1–2h | Mid ($15–25/h) | Any | No | Calm mornings only; wind picks up by noon
- **Kayaking tour** | Nature/Physical | 2–3h | Mid ($20–35 guided) | Family, Friends | Recommended | Guided tours go to sea caves and hidden beaches

### Afternoon (Moderate Energy)
- **Beach hopping by longtail boat / speedboat** | Nature | 3–4h | Mid-Premium ($20–60) | Any | Yes | Charter your own for flexibility; shared tours cheaper
- **Island-hopping day trip** | Nature | Full day | Mid-Premium ($30–80) | Any | Yes | Book via hotel or local operators the day before
- **Jet ski / water sports** | Sport | 1h | Mid ($30–60/h) | Friends, Teens | No | Check insurance and negotiate before payment
- **Cooking class** | Cultural/Food | 3h | Mid ($30–50) | Couple, Friends, Family | Yes | Includes market visit at many venues
- **Pool time + resort** | Relaxation | Flexible | Free/Included | Any | No | Perfect for midday heat (11am–3pm too hot for beach)

### Evening
- **Sunset beach bar** | Food/Entertainment | 2h | Budget-Mid ($5–20) | Friends, Couple | No | Arrive 30 min before sunset for seats
- **Night market / seafood grill** | Food | 2h | Budget ($5–15) | Any | No | Order by weight at seafood grills — agree on price first
- **Fire show / cultural performance** | Entertainment | 1–2h | Free–Mid ($10) | Any | Check schedule | Often on the beach; tip performers
- **Stargazing on beach** | Nature | 1h | Free | Any | No | Best away from resort lights; bring a blanket

### Rainy Day Alternatives (Beach)
- Thai cooking class (indoor)
- Spa day (affordable at beach resorts, budget $20–60/person)
- Local market exploration (most markets are covered)
- Temple or cultural site visit
- Board game café / movie day at resort

## MOUNTAIN / TREKKING ACTIVITIES

### Morning (Peak Energy)
- **Sunrise summit hike** | Physical | 3–6h | Free–Budget ($5 park fee) | Fit adults | No (self-guided) or guide recommended | Start before 5am; bring headlamp; weather clears by 7am
- **Multi-day trek: Day N** | Physical | Full day | Budget-Mid | Adults, Teens | Guide required in some areas | Hire a local guide — supports community, improves safety
- **Waterfall hike** | Nature | 2–4h | Free–Budget | Any except strollers | No | Upper falls usually less crowded; slippery rocks — wear grip shoes
- **Rock climbing (beginner)** | Sport | 3h | Mid ($40–60 with gear) | Adults, fit Teens | Yes | Many mountain towns have climbing schools

### Afternoon
- **Village homestay experience** | Cultural | 2–3h or overnight | Budget ($15–30) | Any | Yes (arrange day before) | Learn to cook local food, meet the community
- **Mountain biking downhill** | Sport | 2–3h | Mid ($25–40 incl. bike) | Adults, Teens | Yes | Downhill from summit — exhilarating, moderate fitness needed
- **Hot spring soak** | Relaxation | 2h | Budget-Mid ($5–20) | Any | No | After a hard hike — legs will thank you; cold plunge optional

### Evening
- **Night market in mountain town** | Food | 2h | Budget ($5–15) | Any | No | Try local mountain produce: wild mushrooms, mountain pork
- **Bonfire + stargazing** | Nature | 2h | Free (bring wood) | Any | No | Altitude = clearer skies; download a star chart app

### Rainy Day Alternatives (Mountain)
- Cave exploration tours (most caves have shelter)
- Local museum or cultural center
- Coffee plantation visit (most have indoor tasting rooms)
- Indoor climbing gym (larger mountain towns)
- Rest day at guesthouse with board games and local meals

## CITY BREAK ACTIVITIES

### Morning
- **Food market tour** | Food | 2–3h | Free–Budget ($5–10 tastings) | Any | Guided recommended | Local markets usually peak 7–10am; try 5 things, don't overeat early
- **Neighborhood walking tour** | Cultural | 2–3h | Free (self) or Mid ($15–25 guided) | Any | No | Download audio guides (Rick Steves, GPSmyCity) for self-guided
- **Major museum visit** | Cultural | 2–4h | Mid ($10–25) | Any (check kid-friendly exhibits) | Yes (skip-the-line tickets essential for top museums) | Go on opening day — fewer crowds
- **Cycling city tour** | Physical/Cultural | 3h | Mid ($20–35 incl. bike) | Adults, Teens | Yes | Flat cities ideal; hilly cities: e-bikes available

### Afternoon
- **Street art or architecture walk** | Cultural | 2h | Free | Any | No | Instagram-worthy without crowds; research key streets beforehand
- **Local neighborhood café hop** | Food | 2h | Budget-Mid ($3–15) | Friends, Couple | No | Ask locals for recommendations — avoid TripAdvisor top lists
- **Shopping: local market or mall** | Shopping | 2–3h | Flexible | Any | No | Local markets: more authentic and cheaper than tourist shops
- **Rooftop bar or observation deck** | Entertainment | 1–2h | Mid-Premium ($15–30) | Adults | Reservation recommended for rooftop bars | Go 1 hour before sunset for best light and no queue

### Evening
- **Dinner at local restaurant (off tourist street)** | Food | 2h | Budget-Mid ($10–30) | Any | Reservation for groups of 6+ | One street behind tourist strips = half the price, double the quality
- **Night food tour** | Food | 3h | Mid ($35–55 guided) | Any | Yes | Combines 6–8 tastings with cultural context from local guide
- **Live music venue** | Entertainment | 2–3h | Budget-Mid ($5–20 cover) | Adults, Teens | Check schedule | Smaller local venues > tourist-oriented bars; ask hotel concierge
- **River or harbor cruise** | Entertainment | 1–2h | Mid ($20–40) | Any | Yes | Dinner cruise available in most port cities; check sunset timing

### Rainy Day Alternatives (City)
- Indoor art gallery or museum
- Cooking class
- Escape room (great for groups)
- Cinema in local language with subtitles
- Shopping mall exploration
- Day spa or hammam

## THEME PARK / ENTERTAINMENT ACTIVITIES

### Morning (Arrive Early)
- **High-priority rides: go at park opening** | Entertainment | 2h | Included in ticket | Any | Yes — buy tickets online, avoid queues | Rope drop strategy: head to most popular ride first, before crowds arrive
- **Character meet-and-greet (for kids)** | Entertainment | 30min each | Included or small fee | Family | Check schedule on app | Schedule on the app and show up 15 min early

### Afternoon
- **Shows and parades** | Entertainment | 1–2h each | Included | Any | Check park schedule | Grab spots 20–30 min before; download park app for live wait times
- **Lower-priority rides** | Entertainment | Flexible | Included | Any | — | Afternoon: crowds shift, some popular rides get shorter queues 2–4pm

### Evening
- **Fireworks / light show** | Entertainment | 30–45min | Included | Any | Reserve viewing spot 1h before | Stake out ground early; park app shows recommended viewing areas

### Practical Theme Park Tips
- Arrive 30 min before opening
- Download the official park app (real-time wait times)
- Buy premium skip-the-line passes for families with kids (worth it)
- Eat breakfast before entering (park food is expensive)
- Pack snacks and refillable water bottles
- Plan a mid-day break (2–4pm) back at hotel if staying nearby — especially for families with young kids
- Rainy day: queue times often drop — great day for usually-long rides
