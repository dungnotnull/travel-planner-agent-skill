## Budget Tier Definitions

| Tier | Daily Spend / Person | Accommodation | Food | Transport | Activities |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Budget | $20–50 | Hostel dorm or guesthouse ($5–20/night) | Street food + markets ($5–15/day) | Public bus, motorbike hire ($3–8/day) | Free sites + cheap tours ($5–15/day) |
| Mid-range | $50–150 | 3-star hotel or Airbnb ($30–80/night) | Local restaurants + occasional splurge ($15–40/day) | Mix public + taxi/Grab ($10–20/day) | Paid attractions + some tours ($15–40/day) |
| Comfort | $150–300 | 4-star hotel or boutique ($80–180/night) | Curated restaurants ($40–80/day) | Private transfers + occasional rental ($25–50/day) | Guided tours, skip-the-line ($40–80/day) |
| Luxury | $300+ | 5-star or villa ($200+/night) | Fine dining + private dining ($80+/day) | Private car/driver ($80+/day) | Private guides, exclusive experiences ($100+/day) |

## Budget Category Breakdown

### Accommodation Formula
```
Base nightly rate (from tier) × destination multiplier × nights = accommodation total
Group saving: 2 people sharing = ~50% saving on per-person cost
```

**Budget examples:**
- Hostel dorm in SE Asia: $8–15/night/person
- 3-star hotel in SE Asia: $25–60/night (room, split between 2 = $12–30/person)
- 4-star hotel in Europe: $120–200/night/room
- 5-star in Maldives: $500–2000/night

**Tip:** Booking.com, Agoda (best for Asia), Airbnb (for groups of 4+, often cheaper per-person)

### Activities & Entrance Fees

**Free & Budget Activities (always list these options):**
- Parks, beaches, scenic viewpoints: Free
- Most religious sites: Free or donation ($1–5)
- Public markets: Free entry
- Self-guided walking tours (download app): Free
- Hiking: Usually free (some national parks charge $5–25 entry)

### Emergency Buffer

Always add 10% to the total as emergency buffer. This covers:
- Medical expenses (minor) not covered by insurance
- Lost items replacement
- Price changes on activities
- Extra nights if flight is delayed
- Unexpected transport costs

## Currency & Payment Tips

### Best Way to Get Local Currency
1. **Preferred:** Use a local ATM with a no-foreign-fee debit card (e.g., Wise, Revolut, Charles Schwab)
2. **Avoid:** Airport currency exchange (worst rates, 5–10% markup)
3. **Avoid:** Hotel exchange desks
4. **Emergency only:** Credit card cash advance (high fees + interest)

### Cash vs Card by Region
| Region | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| SE Asia | Carry cash. Many local places card-not-accepted. ATMs widely available. |
| Japan | Heavily cash-based. 7-Eleven ATMs most reliable for foreign cards. |
| Europe | Mostly card. Tap-to-pay everywhere. Keep €50–100 cash for small vendors. |
| USA | Card preferred everywhere. Tap-to-pay universal. |
| India | Mix. Many places cash-only. UPI for locals. Carry INR. |

### Pre-Trip Money Checklist
- [ ] Notify your bank of travel dates (avoid card being blocked)
- [ ] Load travel money card (Wise/Revolut) with local currency before leaving
- [ ] Check daily ATM withdrawal limits (may need to increase)
- [ ] Know the ATM fee for your card ($2–5/withdrawal typical)
- [ ] Download Wise or Revolut for real-time exchange rates

## Cost-Saving Strategies by Trip Type

### Beach Trip
- Stay just inland from the beach (10–15 min walk = 30–50% cheaper accommodation)
- Buy snacks and drinks at local market/7-Eleven before heading to beach
- Rent gear from dive shop rather than through hotel (usually 30–50% cheaper)

### City Break
- Buy 48–72h city transport pass (usually saves vs individual tickets for 4+ trips/day)
- Book museum tickets online (often discounted vs door price)
- Eat lunch at restaurants, not dinner (same menu, 20–40% cheaper at lunch)
- Look for "happy hour" deals at bars (often 5–7pm)

### Mountain / Trekking
- Hire a local guide rather than agency-packaged tour (negotiate directly; supports community)
- Cook your own meals at guest houses (buy food at village markets)
- Rent gear locally rather than buying or renting at home (cheaper, lighter to travel)

### Large Groups (10+)
- Charter private transport rather than paying individual tickets (usually 20–40% cheaper for 10+)
- Book group accommodation (villa, hostel private floor, guesthouse takeover) — negotiate group rate
- Organize a group dinner at a local restaurant rather than eating separately (negotiate set menu)
- Buy group attraction tickets in advance (most parks and museums have group rates)
